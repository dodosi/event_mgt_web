Maralia
1. Rapid Impact project: Evaluate the impact of e-health in the management of severe malaria cases in Rwanda.

Project outcome: Develop an SMS based system (RapidSMS) to report severe malaria and drug stock out at community level. The severe malaria notification system users are into three groups: 1) reporters who are the CHWs; 2) data viewers and 3) decision makers who are the HC, DH and Ambulance services; and the RBC team who is a constant monitor of the interactions. The central server plays a role of storage and sends notifications and reminders to HCs, DHs and the central level

Before working with this theme, you should become familiar with the Bootstrap framework, especially the utility classes.

COVID-19 PROJECTS
1. Mini-Rapid Impact project: Genomic sequencing of SARS-CoV-2 in Rwanda.

Project outcome: SARS-CoV-2 genome sequencing reported for the 1st time lineage diversity in the country. The study showed a shift (in time) in variant distribution towards the then newly emerging sub-lineage A.23.1. It also allowed detection of the first Rwandan cases of the B.1.1.7 and B.1.351 which were variants of concern, among incoming travelers tested at Kigali International Airport
